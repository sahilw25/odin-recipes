RosOmelette

Recipe for Goan Ros Omelette made with Mushroom Xacuti Gravy


Ros Omelette is a classic Goan breakfast dish. Ros / Ras means gravy. Omelettes are served with leftover gravy from dinner (usually chicken xacuti gravy) and Pav. This is a very popular street food in Goa



Ingredients


  • 2 tablespoon Tomato finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoon onion finely chopped
  • 1 green chilli finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon corriander leaves finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Black Pepper Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon oil
  • To taste Salt
  • 1/2 onion finely chopped
  • 1 lemon chopped into 4 pi

Instructions

Let us prepare the omelette
  1. Take a mixing bowl. Add finely chopped onion, finely chopped tomato, finely chopped coriander leaves and finely chopped green chili.
  2. Add the spice powders-turmeric powder, red chili powder and black pepper powder and mix.
  3. Break the egg and pour it into the bowl. Discard the shell.
  4. Beat the egg and mix well until fluffy.
  5. Add salt to taste and mix well.
  6. Take the frying pan and heat it. Add oil to the pan. Spread it by moving the pan around.
  7. Pour the egg and cook on low flame till omelette is cooked on one side. Fold the sides and gently flip it to the other side and cook till egg is completely cooked.

For Ros
  1. For serving the ros omelette in authentic Goan way, take a steel plate and add the omelette. Pour 1 ladle curry from xacuti. Do not add any chicken pieces.

Serving:
  1. For serving the ros omelette in authentic Goan way, take a steel plate and add the omelette. Pour 1 ladle curry from xacuti. Do not add any chicken pieces.
  2. Garnish with chopped onion and lemon.
  3. Serve with fresh Goan pav.
